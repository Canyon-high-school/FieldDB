LingSync was developed by iLanguage Lab. We are programming field linguists based in Montreal, QC--we were glad to be able to apply our experience as linguists in order to make this tool as useful as possible to others!
The app was developed in collaboration with students and professors of linguistics from local universities (McGill and Concordia)
